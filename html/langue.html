About Amazigh Numbers

The Amazigh number system is an essential part of the Tamazight language. These numbers have been used for centuries by Amazigh people across North Africa. The system is vigesimal (base-20) in its traditional form, though modern usage often adapts to the decimal system.

Additional Language Features

The Amazigh language, also known as Tamazight, is rich in its numerical expressions. Numbers can be combined to form larger values, and there are specific rules for counting different types of objects. The language also includes various numerical prefixes and suffixes that modify the meaning of numbers in different contexts.

The Amazigh number system reflects the deep mathematical understanding of the Amazigh people and their contribution to North African culture and commerce throughout history.
